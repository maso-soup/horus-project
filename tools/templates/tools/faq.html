FAQs

What does Eye of Horus do?

    The Summary functionality gives an overview of your Cardano wallet's Ada balance, rewards, NFT values, token values, and total.
    The Wallet functionality allows for a simple and clean view to admire your NFT collections or visualize what assets are in your Cardano wallet

Why is my wallet taking so long to load?

    Larger wallets or wallets with NFT collections that have a large circulating supply will take longer to load. This is normal and a result of the NFT rarity API being used

How are NFT rarities and token prices determined?

    NFT Rarity is determined from CNFTJungle.io and Token prices are determined from Minswap.org, with all values fetched at runtime to provide the most up to date info

Can I bookmark these pages?

    Yes! The pages are meant to be bookmarked for easy access, i.e. https://eyeofhorus.io/$eyeofhorus or https://eyeofhorus.io/wallet/$eyeofhorus

Who do I tell if I see errors or problems with the site?

    If any errors or incorrect behavior are encountered, please DM @eyeofhorus_io on Twitter

Why don't all of my NFTs or tokens show up in the value table of the Summary page?

    In our value calculation for the wallet we exclude any NFTs or tokens with a zero value, which includes NFTs without a known floor price or tokens not listed on Minswap
